What is Green Architecture?

Renewable Energy Sources

Reducing energy consumption is one of the biggest goals of green architecture, so it follows that these types of constructions will often include a power source that runs on renewable energy. Not every building is a good choice for solar because it does depend on how much sun exposure and surface area you have.

Efficient Systems For Ventilation And Air Quality Control

Efficient ventilation systems are also key in designing a truly green space. Natural ventilation systems, which rely on the natural forces of wind and buoyancy to bring fresh air into the building, may even be used in areas where climate permits.

Use Of Sustainable And Recycled Materials

Green design’s focus on the environment starts before the home is even built. Green architects focus on using non-toxic, recycled and sustainable building materials. This means that the carbon emissions during the manufacturing process – as well as the transportation to distribution centers – are factored into the carbon footprint analysis.

Services I provide

Concept Design

I help you visualize how the build is going to look like before you even decide if you want to count on me for the project! This can help you decide if this is the build you want to make and find things you don't like once you see them.

Feasibility Studies

I make you understand the viability of your development goal for a property. This involves a study of targeted objectives that will affect the development size, cost, and use. Is this where we discuss the project goals, the location, the massing, overall floor plans, unit counts, elevations, etc.

Cost Budget Planning

How much is going to cost? How can we lower the overall cost of the production? How can I finatiate the build? These are the type of questions we'll discuss in this stage. I can recomend you some types of finatiation or maybe you want to change the build plan a little to fit your budget.

Here starts the fun part! We are decided to start the project so I get hands to work by starting the Construction Drawings. These are essential for the building process and ensuring the build is of quality and safe for living.

How much is the budjet?

The budjet depends on you. I am able to work with flexible budjet! From small startups to international companies, I accept all kinds of projects with all kinds of budjet.

Has the Feasibility Study any cost?

I have always compromised myself with doing the feasibility study for free because I think it's important to know just how possible the project is before starting it. Even if you don't end up working with me, I like to make sure you have the best look upon your work.

What maintenance does your buildings need?

I always try to make my buildings so they can last as long as possible, but all builds need some sort of maintenance. My builds don't require any special maintenance, but you should make sure the plants get the care they need, they are the protagonists of the build.
